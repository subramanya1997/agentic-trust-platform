## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What happens if I exceed my limits?**
A: You're automatically billed for overage at the rates listed above. There's no service interruption.

**Q: Can I roll over unused executions?**
A: No, limits reset monthly. Consider upgrading if you consistently underuse.

**Q: What AI models does Agentic Trust support?**
A: Agentic Trust supports the latest AI models from leading providers (as of December 2025):

- **OpenAI**: GPT-5.2 (latest flagship, WebDev excellence), GPT-5.1 (fast reasoning, adaptive thinking), GPT-4.1 (enhanced capabilities, improved performance) - all with 200K context
- **Anthropic**: Claude Opus 4.5 (top coding, agentic tasks, 30+ hour workflows), Claude Sonnet 4.5 (real-world coding, enterprise, safety), Claude Haiku 4.5 (speed, real-time interactions) - all with 200K context
- **Google DeepMind**: Gemini 3 Pro (massive context, multimodal, Deep Think mode), Gemini 2.5 Pro/Flash (long context, creative writing) - up to 1M+ tokens
- **xAI**: Grok 4.1 (real-time data, uncensored, social media) - 256K context
- **Meta**: Llama 4 Maverick (multimodal, long context MoE), Llama 4 Scout (efficient MoE, edge deployment) - open-weight
- **Mistral AI**: Mistral Large 3 (multilingual, multimodal), Mistral Medium 3.1, Ministral 3 (edge AI), Devstral 2 (code generation) - open-weight
- **DeepSeek**: DeepSeek V3.2 (balanced reasoning), DeepSeek V3.2 Speciale (max reasoning, gold-medal math/coding) - open-source MIT license
- **Bring Your Own**: Any compatible model

All pricing is normalized to Claude Sonnet 4.5 equivalents as the unit of measure, so you can use any supported model while maintaining consistent pricing.

**Q: How does pricing work with different models?**
A: All usage is converted to Sonnet 4.5 token equivalents for billing. For example, if you use an open-source model like Llama 4, Mistral Large 3, or DeepSeek V3.2 that costs less per token, you'll get more executions for the same credit. If you use a more expensive model (like GPT-5.2 or Gemini 3 Pro), executions will consume more credits proportionally. This ensures fair pricing regardless of which model you choose.

**Q: Can I downgrade mid-cycle?**
A: Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. No prorated refunds are issued.

**Q: Is there a minimum commitment?**
A: No minimum commitment required. Pay monthly at $3,999/mo or annually (same rate, billed at $47,988/year).

**Q: What's the cancellation policy?**
A: Cancel anytime, effective at the end of the current billing period. Annual plans may be cancelled without penalty, but no prorated refunds are issued for partial periods.

**Q: Do you offer custom enterprise agreements?**
A: Yes. Contact founders@agentictrust.com for volume discounts, custom terms, or unique requirements.
